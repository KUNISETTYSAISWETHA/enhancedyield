Guidelines for farmer

Our project is about predicting the yield of the crop by providing input features such as Crop,crop year, state, area, estimated yield produced, annual rainfall, fertilizer, pesticide, yield.

About Dataset

This dataset encompasses agricultural data for multiple crops cultivated across various states in India from the year 1997 till 2020. The dataset provides crucial features related to crop yield prediction, including crop types, crop years, states, areas under cultivation, production quantities, annual rainfall, fertilizer usage, pesticide usage, and calculated yields.

Attributes Explanation
Investment Enter the amount which is being invested in INR.
Year Enter the year.
Annual_Rainfall The annual rainfall received in the crop-growing region (in mm).
Area The total land area (in hectares) under cultivation for the specific crop
Estimated yield produced The estimated quantity of crop production (in metric tons).
Fertilizer The total amount of fertilizer used for the crop (in kilograms).
Pesticide The total amount of pesticide used for the crop (in kilograms).
Crop The name of the crop cultivated.
State The Indian state where the crop was cultivated.
